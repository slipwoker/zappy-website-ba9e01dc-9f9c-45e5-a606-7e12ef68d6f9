A holistic approach to physiotherapy

Treat pain as information—then respond with clarity.

The work integrates assessment, movement, breathing, and the mental-emotional context that can amplify symptoms. Rather than treating pain as an isolated mechanical issue, this approach recognizes the interconnectedness of body and mind in the healing process. Every session is designed to address not just the physical manifestation of discomfort, but the underlying patterns and stressors that contribute to chronic pain.

Through careful listening and observation, the physiotherapy treatment focuses on understanding your unique pain signals and responding with evidence-based techniques tailored to your individual needs. This holistic method combines manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, breathwork, and mindfulness practices to create a comprehensive path toward lasting relief and improved quality of life.

Listening comes first

Pain is not an enemy; it is a signal that deserves interpretation.

We consider load, sleep, stress, breathing, and daily habits.

The goal is sustainable change, not short-term fixes.

How the process feels

1

Assessment

We begin with a thorough conversation about your history, what brings you here, and what your body is telling you. Through gentle tests and careful observation, we gain clarity about the root of your discomfort—not just the symptoms, but the underlying patterns that need attention.

2

Plan

Together, we create a tailored movement and recovery plan that respects your body's pace. This isn't a one-size-fits-all protocol—it's a personalized roadmap that integrates what you can do today, with gradual progressions that build strength and confidence without overwhelming your system.

3

Accompaniment

Throughout your healing journey, you're never alone. I provide ongoing support, making adjustments as your body responds and changes. This continuous accompaniment builds not just physical recovery, but the confidence and understanding you need to maintain your progress independently.

You leave with a plan you can repeat and understand—not just exercises, but a deeper connection to your body's wisdom and capacity for healing.
